Lily - Symbol of Purity

The Lily is often associated with purity, virtue, and renewal. Its elegant, trumpet-shaped flowers are admired for their beauty and fragrance. In various cultures, lilies symbolize different things, such as motherhood in ancient Greece and Christianity, where the white lily represents the Virgin Mary. With their stunning blooms, lilies are popular in weddings and formal events. Lilies come in many colors, each representing distinct emotions, such as white for purity, pink for admiration, and yellow for joy. These perennials are also known for their ability to grow back every year, symbolizing hope and rebirth.
